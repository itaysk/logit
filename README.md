# Logly - iOS routine tracking app

Logly helps you keep track of your routines, habits, chores, goals and more. 

- Simple and functional - One tap to log.
- Multi-device support, seamless iCloud syncing.
- Share a routine with someone and track together!
- Automate via Apple Shortcuts.

Use cases:
- When was the last time you did something (did I water the plant yesterday?)
- How often something happens? (am I having that stomach ache often?)
- Counter (how many 3 point that player made during the game?)
- Who did something? (Is it myt turn to take out the trash?)
- Are you keeping up with your goals? (Do I read at least 3 times a week?)
